Privacy Policy

English

in. does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • All records you create in the app are stored only on your device and in the shared local storage used by the widget.
  • The app has no user accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking.
  • The app connects to Apple’s App Store only for in-app purchases and purchase restoration. Your in-app records are not sent to Apple.
  • If you delete the app, all data stored by the app is permanently deleted.
  • There is no data to sell, share, or lose.
