FAQ

Q. Who can use CHARA?

A. You must be at least 18 years old to use CHARA. An adult may enter information about another person, including a child in their care, only when authorized to do so.

Q. What information is used to generate a name?

A. CHARA sends the nationality, gender, age, height, weight, personality answers, and app language you provide to Google Gemini through our Supabase-hosted service. CHARA asks for your consent before the first generation.

Q. How should I use the generated names?

A. CHARA uses AI to create personalized suggestions for entertainment and creative inspiration. Because AI can produce uncommon combinations or readings, review a name carefully before using it outside the app.

Q. Why is name generation unavailable?

A. Confirm that your device is online, then try again after a short wait. Daily and service-wide safety limits may temporarily prevent generation. If the problem continues, contact us with the exact message shown in the app.

Q. How do rewarded ads and credits work?

A. When an unlock is required, you can choose to watch an available rewarded ad or use one credit. A reward is granted only after the ad provider confirms completion. Ad availability varies by region and time.

Q. What should I do if a purchase is not reflected?

A. Purchases are processed by Apple. If Apple shows a completed purchase but CHARA did not add the credits, contact us with the purchase date and product. Do not send payment card or bank details. For refunds and billing issues, visit Apple Support for billing.

Q. Can I restore credits on another device?

A. Credits are consumable and stored on your device. They are not guaranteed to transfer to another device or return after the app is deleted.

Q. How can I withdraw AI consent?

A. Open the app menu and select Revoke AI Consent. CHARA will ask again before sending new information to Google Gemini. Revocation does not delete information already processed or stored.
